ORDERS

Malaka Spice, Koregaon Park is the flagship restaurant. Established as a small restaurant in Koregoan Park, Pune in 1997, this restaurant has grown massively in size and fans. You will find the oldest patrons dining here under the familiar ambience of the Badam Tree, surrounded by chirping sparrows and easels year after year.
